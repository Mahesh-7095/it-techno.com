HTML Quotations

Blockquote

The <blockquote> tag is used for long quotations. 


<blockquote>
    This is a long quotation. It is often displayed with indentation.
</blockquote>
This is a long quotation. It is often displayed with indentation.

Inline Quotation

The <q> tag is used for short, inline quotations. 


<q>This is a short quotation.</q>
This is a short quotation.

Citation

The <cite> tag is used to reference the title of a work. 


<cite>The Great Gatsby</cite> by F. Scott Fitzgerald.
The Great Gatsby by F. Scott Fitzgerald.

Abbreviation

The <abbr> tag is used for abbreviations.


<abbr title="World Health Organization">WHO</abbr>
WHO
