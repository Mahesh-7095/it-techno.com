Welcome to Cybersecurity!

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. These attacks are usually aimed at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive information, extorting money, or interrupting normal business processes.

What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity involves implementing measures to protect computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from malicious attacks. It is essential for individuals, businesses, and governments to safeguard their digital assets.

Why is Cybersecurity Important?

  • Protects Sensitive Data: Prevents unauthorized access to personal and financial information.
  • Ensures Business Continuity: Protects against disruptions caused by cyberattacks.
  • Builds Trust: Enhances customer and stakeholder confidence.

Key Topics in Cybersecurity

  • Network Security
  • Application Security
  • Information Security
  • Endpoint Security
  • Cloud Security
