HTML Video

Embedding Video

Use the <video> tag to embed video content in HTML. 


<video controls width="100%">
    <source src="video.mp4" type="video/mp4">
    Your browser does not support the video element.
</video>

Video Attributes

You can customize video playback using attributes like autoplay, loop, and muted. 


<video controls autoplay loop muted width="100%">
    <source src="video.mp4" type="video/mp4">
    Your browser does not support the video element.
</video>
