Welcome to Artificial Intelligence!

Artificial Intelligence (AI) involves creating systems that can perform tasks requiring human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, and problem-solving.

What is AI?

AI is the simulation of human intelligence in machines. It includes subfields like machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision.

Why Learn AI?

  • Innovation: Drive innovation in various industries.
  • Automation: Automate repetitive tasks.
  • Problem Solving: Solve complex problems using data-driven insights.
