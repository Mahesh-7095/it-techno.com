HTML Tables

Basic Table

Use the <table>, <tr>, <th>, and <td> tags to create a table. 


<table>
    <tr>
        <th>Name</th>
        <th>Age</th>
        <th>City</th>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>John Doe</td>
        <td>25</td>
        <td>New York</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>Jane Smith</td>
        <td>30</td>
        <td>Los Angeles</td>
    </tr>
</table>
Name Age City
John Doe 25 New York
Jane Smith 30 Los Angeles
