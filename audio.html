HTML Audio

Embedding Audio

Use the <audio> tag to embed audio content in HTML. 


<audio controls>
    <source src="audio.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">
    Your browser does not support the audio element.
</audio>

Audio Attributes

You can customize audio playback using attributes like autoplay, loop, and muted. 


<audio controls autoplay loop muted>
    <source src="audio.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">
    Your browser does not support the audio element.
</audio>
