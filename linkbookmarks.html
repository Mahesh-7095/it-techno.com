Creating Bookmarks

Bookmarks allow you to link to specific sections of a webpage. Use the id attribute to create a bookmark and the href attribute with # to link to it. 


<h2 id="section1">Section 1</h2>
<p>This is the content of section 1.</p>
<a href="#section1">Go to Section 1</a>
