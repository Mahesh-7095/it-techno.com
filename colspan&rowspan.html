HTML Table Colspan & Rowspan

Using Colspan & Rowspan

The colspan attribute merges cells horizontally, and the rowspan attribute merges cells vertically. 


<table>
    <tr>
        <th colspan="2">Name</th>
        <th>Age</th>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td rowspan="2">John Doe</td>
        <td>25</td>
        <td>New York</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>30</td>
        <td>Los Angeles</td>
    </tr>
</table>
